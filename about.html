Who We Are

Agricultural Recovery Specialists

When the USDA rolled out the Emergency Relief Program (ERP), over 218,000 producers received Track 1 payments but never applied for Track 2. Why? Because Track 1 was automatic — no application required. Track 2 required producers to file an application, and most never knew it existed.

Here's what they missed: Track 2 payments were routinely 10x higher than Track 1. Billions of dollars went unclaimed simply because no one told these farmers they were eligible or helped them apply.

When you work with our firm, we proactively identify every program you qualify for — including ones you've never heard of — then maximize your payments through expert knowledge of every rule in the FSA program handbooks. Think of us like a CPA who knows the tax code inside and out — we know USDA disaster relief program handbooks in and out.

Our team includes former Farm Service Agency (FSA) staff, policy analysts, and agricultural specialists who work collaboratively with local FSA offices to ensure your applications are complete, accurate, and positioned for maximum benefit.

What we do for you:

  • Maximize your funds through expert handbook knowledge
  • Save your time — we handle all paperwork and complexity
  • Eliminate headaches — full-service disaster relief specialists
  • We proactively seek out USDA programs you qualify for — especially the ones that come out with short notice and are only around for 30 days (like the MASC)
  • We let you focus on farming — no more spending time at government offices
